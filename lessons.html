Business English

This course is created for those who are planning on working in a business environment and also with foreign clients. Students will expand their business vocabulary and also increase their knowledge of key business aspects. The duration of the course is three month and all of the textbooks will be provided.

English for exams

This is a unique course that is designed for everyone who is willing to take Cambridge exams. This course offers a great variety of exams including Flyers,KET,PET,CAE,FCE and IELTS. Students will have an opportunity to improve their language skills and also get familiar with the exam's procedure, moreover they will get an access to free materials provided by the tutor. Duration of the course depends on the student.

15$ per hour.
